Samosa

Flaky and crunchy fried Samosa are one of the most popular street food snack in North Indian cuisine. They feature a pastry-like crust but are filled with savory and spiced potato and green peas for a hearty, delicious snack. This step-by-step recipe guide will help you to make the flakiest, tastiest, absolutely best Punjabi Samosa or Aloo Samosa from scratch!

Ingredients you need

    Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • Filling:

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup mixed boiled vegetables
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 2 green chilies, ginger
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Spices: coriander, garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, salt
  • Oil for frying

How to make Samosa

  1. Mix 2 cups flour, 1/4 cup oil, water, and 1/2 tsp salt. Rest for 30 mins.
  2. Saut√© 1/2 cup onions, 2 chilies, ginger, cumin. Add 2 cups mashed potatoes, 1 cup veggies, and spices. Garnish with cilantro.
  3. Roll dough, form semi-circles. Fill, seal into triangles.
  4. Fry until golden brown
